Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

Applications for the maiden edition will close strictly on the date announced on our timeline. Late entries will not be accepted via the Tech-IDEAS portal.

Does it cost money to apply?

No. The Tech-Innovation Challenge is completely free for all eligible Nigerian innovators.

Do I need a fully functional product?

A Minimum Viable Product (MVP) or a strongly validated prototype is required to pass the initial screening stage.
