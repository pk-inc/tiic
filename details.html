The TICC Journey

A structured progression identifying the most viable innovations by engaging industry experts.

Phase 1

Application & Screening

National call for entries. Rigorous review of technical viability and market potential.

Phase 2

Top 30 Selection

Initial shortlist. Deep-dive interviews and prototype assessment.

Phase 3

Top 15 Bootcamp

Intensive venture building, refinement, and pitch preparation.

Phase 4

Finalists Selection

Selection of the most promising ventures ready for investment.

Phase 5

Grand Finale & Demo Day

Held on December 21, 2026 at the Local Content Towers (NCDMB HQ) in Yenagoa, Bayelsa. Final pitch event before industry experts, investment allocations, and awards.

Post-Challenge Support

01.

Incubation

Structured residency at the EIC with access to technical resources and workspace.

02.

Acceleration

Intensive go-to-market strategy and growth execution support.

03.

Funding

Direct equity-free grants and facilitated introductions to angel networks.

04.

Visibility

National press coverage and showcase opportunities at major tech summits.

Focus Areas

  • Agritech & Food Security
  • Healthtech & Biotech
  • Edtech & Future of Work
  • Fintech & Financial Inclusion
  • Cleantech & Renewable Energy

Eligibility

  • Must be a registered student or recent graduate in Nigeria.
  • Solution must leverage technology to solve a localized problem.
  • Must have at least a working prototype or MVP.
  • Commitment to attend the bootcamp in person if selected.

Why Participate

  • Validate your solution with expert feedback.
  • Gain credibility through an official institutional programme.
  • Accelerate your path to investment readiness.
