About TICC

The Technology Innovation Challenge Competition (TICC) is a premium NCDMB program, organized in partnership with the Energy Researches Innovators Network (ERIN) Committee and partner organisations. As the maiden edition, TICC 2026 is designed to discover, accelerate, and elevate Nigeria's most promising technology solutions through intensive technical workshops, technology exhibitions, and direct engagement with leading industry experts.

Industry Experts

Direct engagement, vetting, and feedback from top-tier industry experts and mentors.

NCDMB Support

A flagship NCDMB program offering unmatched market linkages and strategic local content support.

Maiden Cohort

Be part of the historic first cohort laying the blueprint for Nigerian technology scaling.

Vision

To establish a sustainable pipeline of high-impact technology ventures that strengthen local content and drive national economic and energy transformation.

Mission

To rigorously identify, equip, and accelerate the growth of exceptional local innovators by engaging industry experts through structured, challenge-based competition.
