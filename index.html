About TIC

The Tech-Innovation Challenge (TIC) is a premium NCDMB program, organized to discover, accelerate, and elevate Nigeria's most promising technology solutions. Through intensive technical workshops, technology exhibitions, and direct engagement with leading industry experts, TIC bridges the gap between local innovators and energy industry demands.
