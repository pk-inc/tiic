About TIIC

The Tech Innovation & Investment Challenge is an official programme designed to discover and elevate Nigeria's most promising technical innovators.

Mentorship

Direct guidance from industry veterans and academic experts.

Funding

Access to seed capital for the most viable innovations.

Network

Connect with a national ecosystem of founders and investors.

Vision

To establish a sustainable pipeline of high-impact technology ventures emerging from the Nigerian academic ecosystem, driving national economic transformation.

Mission

To rigorously identify, equip, and accelerate the growth of exceptional innovators through structured challenge-based learning and strategic investment.

Why TIIC

Rigorous Selection

A merit-based, transparent process ensuring only the highest potential ventures advance.

Academic Foundation

Backed by the robust institutional support of Rivers State University's EIC.

Actionable Outcomes

Focus on demonstrable prototypes, market validation, and scalable business models.

Message from the EIC

"The Tech Innovation & Investment Challenge is more than a competition; it is a critical intervention in our national innovation ecosystem. We are committed to identifying the brightest technical minds and providing the rigorous institutional support necessary to transform their prototypes into sustainable enterprises."
- Director, Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre (EIC)
