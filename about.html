About the Tech-Innovation Challenge

Building Nigeria's next generation of energy innovators.

What is TIC?

The Tech-Innovation Challenge (TIC) is a flagship initiative of the Nigerian Content Development & Monitoring Board (NCDMB). It is designed to rigorously identify, equip, and accelerate the growth of exceptional local innovators by engaging industry experts through structured, challenge-based competition. TIC serves to establish a sustainable pipeline of high-impact technology ventures that strengthen local content and drive national economic and energy transformation.

Programme Objectives

Identify Innovators

Discover high-potential local tech solutions.

Build Capacity

Equip participants through expert workshops and mentorship.

Accelerate Solutions

Fast-track commercialization and market entry.

Strengthen Local Content

Enhance indigenous technological capabilities in the energy sector.

Promote Sustainability

Ensure long-term impact and environmental stewardship.

Impact Vision

Our vision is to transform the Nigerian energy landscape by fostering a culture of innovation, where homegrown solutions solve industry-wide challenges, reducing foreign dependency and creating long-lasting economic value.

Programme Ownership

TIC is a fully-owned initiative of the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB), serving as the cornerstone of its technology capability development strategy.

Implementation Partner

The program is proudly powered and implemented by the Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre Ltd (EICL), responsible for the end-to-end execution, participant mobilization, and incubation of selected ventures.
