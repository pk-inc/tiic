# Prompt for Google Antigravity — TIIC Website

Build a marketing/informational website for TIIC (Tech Innovation & Investment Challenge), a national innovation competition in Nigeria organised by the Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre (EIC) at Rivers State University. TIIC 2026 is the maiden edition.

## Tone and positioning
This is an official programme run by an academic institution, not a startup or VC. It should feel like a serious, credible national competition with an application process and a real calendar — closer to a university admissions/programme site than a tech-startup landing page. Confident and substantive, not flashy.

## Design constraints — avoid these defaults
- Do NOT use a navy + gold color combination
- Do NOT use a dotted/connected "ascending pathway" graphic in the hero
- Do NOT represent the competition stages as numbered circles (01, 02, 03...)
- Do NOT use Space Grotesk paired with Inter
- Do NOT use abstract gradient blobs or glassmorphism in the hero

## Design direction
- Pick one bold, slightly unexpected display typeface (e.g. a grotesque like General Sans/Söhne, or a slab serif) paired with a utility/mono face for dates, stage labels, and numbers — lean into an "official programme document" feel.
- Color: one strong ink color (deep green, deep red-brown, or near-black) on a warm/paper background. One accent color used sparingly, only for "Apply" CTAs and deadline dates.
- Signature visual for the competition funnel: TIIC narrows from applicants → top 30 → top 15 → finalists/winners. Represent this literally as a shrinking/funnel shape or stacked horizontal bars of decreasing width — this is the real shape of the competition and should anchor the page.
- Timeline section (June 2026 – Jan 2027): present as a dated programme calendar/ledger — rows with month + milestone, table-like — rather than a vertical icon timeline with dots and connecting lines.
- Stages of "The TIIC Journey" should be presented as labeled programme phases (e.g. "Phase 1 — Application & Screening") rather than numbered circular badges.

## Site structure
1. Header/nav: logo, nav links (About, The Journey, Focus Areas, Eligibility, Timeline, Contact), Apply Now button
2. Hero: "Innovate. Build. Scale." / "The First of Many" + intro copy + Apply Now / Learn More CTAs
3. About TIIC: what it is + benefits list
4. Vision & Mission
5. Why TIIC (pillars)
6. The TIIC Journey: 5 phases with outcomes (funnel visual as anchor)
7. Post-Challenge Support: Incubation, Acceleration, Funding, Visibility
8. TIIC 2026 Timeline (calendar/ledger style, June 2026 – Jan 2027)
9. Why Participate / Eligibility / Focus Areas
10. Message from EIC
11. Final CTA: Apply Now / Become a Partner / Contact Us
12. Footer
