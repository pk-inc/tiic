Programme Structure

The multi-stage journey from application to incubation.

Stage 1

Screening & Selection

Rigorous evaluation of all applications by our panel of experts to identify the most viable and innovative solutions.

Output Top 30 Innovators
Stage 2

Online Pitching

The top 30 innovators present their solutions virtually to a judging panel, defending their technical and commercial viability.

Output 15 Finalists
Stage 3A

Mentorship Phase

Finalists are paired with seasoned industry professionals for one-on-one guidance to refine their business models.

Output Bootcamp Readiness
Stage 3B

The Bootcamp

An immersive, intensive physical accelerator program focusing on rapid capability building and final pitch preparation.

Output Pitch Readiness
Stage 4

Grand Finale

The ultimate showdown at the NCDMB Headquarters. Finalists pitch live to investors, industry leaders, and the ERIN Committee.

Output Winner Selection

Post-Competition Incubation

The journey doesn't end at the finals. Winning teams enter an exclusive incubation program designed to transition prototypes into fully commercialized, market-ready enterprises.

Funding Support

Access to seed capital and venture grants to finance initial operations and scale technology deployment.

Scale Support

Market linkage facilitation, providing direct entry into the supply chains of major energy corporations.

NCDMB Headquarters, Yenagoa, Bayelsa State
